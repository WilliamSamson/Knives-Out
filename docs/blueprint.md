# **App Name**: Knives Out

## Core Features:

- Game Room & Player Management: Create private game rooms, invite 6 players, and manage player statuses within the game.
- Dynamic Character Assignment: Each player is assigned a unique character with a subtly AI-generated backstory and relationships that inherently make them a suspect in the murder.
- Procedural Mystery Generation (AI Tool): A generative AI tool creates a unique murder scenario for each game, detailing the victim's death, crafting complex character alibis and hidden motives, and subtly scattering minimal clues to challenge player deduction.
- Interactive Interrogation System: Players can interrogate each other in real-time, asking questions to uncover generated alibis, motives, and hidden information.
- Evidence Board & Deduction Interface: A shared in-game interface where players can collectively review and organize 'clues' or pieces of information gathered, facilitating collaborative deduction.
- Accusation & Voting Phase: A dedicated phase allowing players to discuss theories, accuse suspects, and cast their final vote for who they believe committed the murder.
- Murder Resolution & Narrative Reveal (AI): The AI tool orchestrates the grand reveal, exposing the true killer and providing a tailored narrative recap based on the game's unique scenario, confirming whether the players successfully solved the mystery.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Indigo (#2E264D). This rich, dark hue provides a sense of profound mystery and elegance, evoking the intensity of a crime thriller.
- Background color: Dark Slate (#17151A). A very dark, desaturated shade of the primary hue, providing a brooding backdrop that emphasizes the mysterious and enclosed setting of the game.
- Accent color: Vibrant Blue (#26A2D3). A vivid and analogous blue hue that stands out sharply against the darker primary and background, used for interactive elements, highlights, and critical information, symbolizing moments of clarity amidst confusion.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern serif for an elegant, high-end feel befitting a sophisticated mystery. Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif chosen for its modern look and excellent readability in continuous text, ensuring clarity for game narratives and dialogue.
- Utilize minimalist line-art icons. Designs will be subtly themed around classic detective imagery such as magnifying glasses, silhouettes, cryptic symbols, and discreet communication elements to reinforce the thriller aesthetic without distracting from the mystery.
- Employ a dark, minimalist interface focusing on clear content hierarchy. Strategic use of spacing and modal overlays will manage the flow of information, maintaining an atmosphere of focused investigation and avoiding visual clutter, akin to a sophisticated game board.
- Subtle and purposeful transitions will guide players between game phases and interaction points. Animations will be understated, designed to provide feedback and reinforce game progression without breaking the immersive and serious tone of the mystery.
